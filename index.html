Common Queries

1. What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the ICSE curriculum, a rigorous and internationally respected system that builds strong foundations in English, Mathematics and Science while encouraging analytical thinking and creativity. ICSE is widely recognised across India and accepted internationally.

2. How many students are in each class?

Our core policy is a maximum of 25 students per class. This low student–teacher ratio ensures personalised attention and meaningful teacher-student engagement — a key element of our premium education model.

3. Do students need tuition after school?

We believe learning should happen at school. At Photon, homework, practice and revision are completed under teacher supervision. We intentionally discourage external tuition — if a child still needs tuition, we must improve our in-school teaching.

4. What are your teachers like?

Our teachers are qualified, experienced, and passionate mentors. Led by Principal Mrs. Renu, they focus on both academic excellence and character development. Regular professional training keeps our team up-to-date with modern teaching practices.

5. How safe is the school?

Student safety is our top priority. The campus features CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, controlled access, regular safety drills, and clean RO drinking water. Classrooms are spacious, well-ventilated and smart-board enabled.

6. What facilities do you offer?

Photon provides smart classrooms, science & computer labs, RO water, CCTV, sports, creative arts and safe transport. We aim for balanced academic and co-curricular development.
